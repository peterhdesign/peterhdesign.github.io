Doo-Doo Double Takes

Role

Public Art

Photography

Tools

Adobe Illustrator

Cricut Design Space

The goal of this public art project was to integrate art into people's everyday lives as a pleasant surprise.

I wanted the art to interrupt people's everyday lives and routines in a beautiful way.

So, why not bring beauty to the some of the most familiar, yet crappy places?

By placing my photography on bathroom signs, I am able to make people do a double take to look at the bathroom signs that should be familiar to them. Whether it is to make sure that they are entering the right bathroom, or just to acknowledge that something is different about the signs, people are forced to look at the art. As a result, this project was cleverly named "Doo-Doo Double Takes."
