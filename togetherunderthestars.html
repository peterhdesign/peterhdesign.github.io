Together Under the Stars

Role

Art Direction

Graphic Design

Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Illustrator

Adobe InDesign

Together Under the Stars is a record album produced to raise awareness about racial hostility and prejudice in America. It also encourages love and camaraderie amongst Americans that has been negatively affected due to racial prejudice.

The artwork, created in Illustrator and Photoshop, is inspired by classic diner, drive-in theater, and motel signs that are unmistakably “American.” T-shirts and pins were also created to further promote the record.
